Avocado Toast - Recipe

I love avocado toast. It's super delicious and easy to make. Below is a simple and quick recipe for making an awesome breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • bread of your choice
  • half an avocado
  • half a lime
  • cayanne pepper
  • sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Toast 2 pieces of bread
  2. slice up half an avocado
  3. spread avocado onto toasted bread
  4. sprinkle on cayenne pepper and sea salt to taste
  5. squeeze on lime juice
  6. voila!
